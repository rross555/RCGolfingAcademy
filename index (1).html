The Story

Raised on the same golfing soil as Rory McIlroy.

Rian grew up in Northern Ireland — a small place that punches absurdly above its weight in world golf, and the home of Rory McIlroy. That's where his game was built: links wind, tight lies, and a culture that treats golf as something you earn shot by shot.

He's carried that game across the Atlantic to Coquitlam, where he now coaches golfers of every level across Greater Vancouver. A single-figure player himself, Rian teaches the fundamentals that actually move your handicap — not gimmicks, just a repeatable swing and a clear plan.

Whether you've never held a club or you're chasing your first single-figure season, you'll get honest, patient coaching from someone who plays the game he teaches.

— Rian CaldwellGolf Coach · Coquitlam, BC

The Approach

No gimmicks. Just better golf.

01

See where you're at

We start with an honest look at your swing, contact and short game — so we're fixing the things that actually cost you shots.

02

Build the fundamentals

Grip, set-up, motion. Get the foundations right and everything downstream gets easier. Simple cues you can actually feel.

03

Take it to the course

A clear practice plan and on-course thinking, so the range work shows up in your scores — not just your warm-up.
